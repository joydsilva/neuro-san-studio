# E&S Wholesale - Contract - Commercial Property

## Commercial Property Coverages and Policy Options
Commercial property products are offered in all states through our E&S Wholesale - Contract division.

- **Commercial Property**: Primary property coverage for buildings, contents, and business personal property
- **Crime**: Protection against employee dishonesty, forgery, theft, and other criminal acts
- **Equipment Breakdown**: Coverage for mechanical and electrical equipment failures and breakdowns
- **Excess Property**: Additional property coverage limits above underlying primary policies
- **Inland Marine**: Specialized coverage for movable property, goods in transit, and specialized equipment
- **Participating Policies**: Policies that may provide dividends or other participation features
- **Primary / Loss Limit**: Flexible policy structures with various deductible and limit options

## Commercial Property Programs
We offer a variety of programs for many classes of business, as well as an abundance of non-program commercial classes. Some of our top program performers include:

- **Artisan contractors**: Specialized property coverage for skilled trade contractors and craftspeople
- **Auto service risks**: Property coverage for automotive repair shops, dealerships, and service centers
- **Builders risk**: Coverage for properties under construction, renovation, or rehabilitation
- **Distributors/Wholesalers**: Comprehensive property coverage for distribution centers and wholesale operations
- **Dwellings**: Property coverage for residential rental properties and multi-family housing
- **Lessor's risk**: Coverage for property owners who lease their buildings to tenants
- **Manufacturing**: Specialized coverage for manufacturing facilities and operations
- **Restaurant/Deli**: Property coverage tailored to food service establishments
- **Selected mercantile**: Coverage for retail establishments and mercantile operations
- **Vacant building**: Specialized coverage for unoccupied properties and buildings
